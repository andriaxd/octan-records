Deep Hypnotic Techno

Octan Records Artist

Peralta

Colombian–Miami born artist exploring the deeper, hypnotic side of techno. His productions revolve around stripped-back rhythms, evolving textures, and atmospheric depth, built with the dance floor in mind. Balancing functionality with subtle sonic exploration, his work moves between hypnotic repetition and restrained sound design. His music has been released on labels such as Subsist, Octan, and Malanoche, receiving support from artists including Oscar Mulero, Claudio PRC, and Mathys Lenne.
