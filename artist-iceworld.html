Octan Records Artist

Iceworld

Iceworld is the stage name of Andria Gonjilashvili, A young Tbilisi based upcoming Producer and DJ. His name stems from the choice of delivering ice cold, reverb filled echoing landscapes. His sets and production never stick to only one genre, tempo or idea, Moreso filling a spectrum of many wide sounds. He has played at venues in Tbilisi such as TES, Left Bank and others. Andria never seizes to improve and perform in a way that sends chills down the spine of listeners.
