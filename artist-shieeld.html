Octan Records Artist

Shieeld

Shieeld — born and raised in Tbilisi, Georgia, now based in Warsaw — is a nature and sound explorer, collector. His artistic vision is rooted in the relationship between natural environments and electronic sound, shaping a distinctive approach to hypnotic deep techno and experimental sound design. Through both DJ sets and productions, he builds immersive sonic journeys where deep, hypnotic rhythms merge with evolving atmospheres and layered soundscapes. Drawing inspiration from field recordings and organic sounds from nature, his work weaves mysterious ancient textures with subtle environmental elements, transforming them into meditative electronic forms. His music remains an ongoing search for hidden frequencies — where nature, experimentation, and hypnotic techno converge. As David Lynch once said: “I like things that leave some mystery to be explored.”
