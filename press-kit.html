About Octan Records

Octan Records is a record label based in Tbilisi, Georgia, focused on delivering deep, experimental, slow hypnotic sounds. Founded in 2026, the label curates a unique sonic experience that transcends boundaries and explores the depths of electronic music.

Our roster includes talented artists like Iceworld, Sebastian Peralta, and Shieeld, each bringing their unique vision to the label's aesthetic of atmospheric, immersive soundscapes.

Artist Bios

Iceworld

Iceworld crafts frozen soundscapes that transport listeners to otherworldly dimensions. Known for deep, hypnotic textures and experimental approaches to sound design, Iceworld's productions blur the lines between ambient, experimental, and deep electronic music. Based in Tbilisi, Georgia.

Sebastian Peralta

Sebastian Peralta blends deep, hypnotic rhythms with dreamy atmospheres. His productions are characterized by slow-burning builds and immersive sonic journeys. Hailing from Buenos Aires, Argentina, Sebastian brings a unique Latin American perspective to the deep techno and hypnotic sound.

Shieeld

Shieeld creates protective sonic barriers through layered soundscapes and experimental production techniques. A master of atmosphere and tension, Shieeld's work explores the darker corners of electronic music with precision and depth. Based in Berlin, Germany.
